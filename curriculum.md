# Lingua Arena — English Curriculum Design
**Platform:** lingua-arena.org  
**Target:** Korean learners of English  
**Framework:** CEFR (A1 → C2)  
**Philosophy:** "언어도 훈련이다 — 매일 조금씩, 퍼즐처럼"

---

## Overview

| Level | Label | Description (KO) | Units | Questions/Unit |
|-------|-------|-------------------|-------|----------------|
| A1 | Beginner | 기초 생존 영어 | 8 | 20–25 |
| A2 | Elementary | 일상 의사소통 | 10 | 20–25 |
| B1 | Intermediate | 친숙한 주제 표현 | 12 | 25–30 |

**Total target (English, Phase 1):** ~60 units / ~1,500 questions

## A1 — Beginner (기초 생존 영어)

### Learning Goals
- 기본 인사, 자기소개
- 숫자, 날짜, 시간, 색깔
- 단순 현재 시제 (be동사, 일반동사)
- 간단한 명사/형용사 어휘 200단어
- 짧은 문장 이해 및 생성

### Core Grammar
| Topic | Examples |
|-------|---------|
| be동사 현재형 | I am, You are, He is |
| 관사 | a, an, the |
| 단수/복수 명사 | cat / cats, box / boxes |
| 인칭대명사 | I, you, he, she, it, we, they |
| 지시대명사 | this, that, these, those |
| 단순 현재 (일반동사) | I like, She likes |
| 의문문 기초 | What is this? / Are you…? |
| There is / There are | There is a book on the table. |

### Core Vocabulary (200 words, grouped by unit)

| Unit | Theme | Sample Words |
|------|-------|-------------|
| U01 | Greetings & Introductions | hello, name, nice, meet, please, thank, sorry |
| U02 | Numbers & Counting | one–twenty, hundred, thousand, how many |
| U03 | Colors & Shapes | red, blue, green, circle, square, big, small |
| U04 | Family & People | mother, father, brother, sister, friend, old, young |
| U05 | Food & Drink | water, bread, apple, coffee, eat, drink, hungry |
| U06 | Places & Directions | school, home, left, right, near, far, go |
| U07 | Time & Days | Monday–Sunday, morning, today, now, when |
| U08 | Body & Health | head, hand, sick, doctor, feel, hurt |

### Puzzle Types Used at A1
- **Multiple Choice (객관식)** — 4-choice word/meaning match
- **Word Order (순서 배열)** — drag words to form a sentence
- **Fill in the Blank (빈칸 채우기)** — choose correct word form
- **Listen & Choose (듣기 선택)** — Web Speech API reads, learner picks meaning
- **Matching (짝 맞추기)** — connect English word to Korean meaning

### Sample Questions — A1

**Q1** `en_a1_u01_q001`  
Type: `multiple_choice`  
> What does "Nice to meet you" mean?  
> A) 만나서 반갑습니다 ✓  
> B) 잘 지내세요?  
> C) 안녕히 가세요  
> D) 감사합니다  
Explanation (EN): A common phrase used when meeting someone for the first time.  
설명 (KO): 처음 만날 때 쓰는 인사 표현입니다.

---

**Q2** `en_a1_u03_q004`  
Type: `word_order`  
> Arrange: [is / The / ball / red]  
> Answer: **The ball is red.**  
Explanation (EN): Subject + be verb + adjective is the basic sentence pattern.  
설명 (KO): '주어 + be동사 + 형용사'는 가장 기본적인 문장 구조입니다.

---

**Q3** `en_a1_u07_q012`  
Type: `fill_in_the_blank`  
> "Today _____ Monday."  
> A) are  B) am  C) **is** ✓  D) be  
Explanation (EN): "Today" is singular, so we use "is."  
설명 (KO): Today(오늘)는 단수이므로 be동사 'is'를 사용합니다.

## A2 — Elementary (일상 의사소통)

### Learning Goals
- 쇼핑, 교통, 여행 등 일상 상황 대화
- 과거/미래 시제 도입
- 조동사 can, must, should 기초
- 어휘 500단어 (A1 포함 누적)
- 짧은 텍스트(이메일, 문자) 이해

### Core Grammar
| Topic | Examples |
|-------|---------|
| 단순 과거 (규칙/불규칙) | walked / went, ate, saw |
| 미래 (will / going to) | I will call. / I'm going to study. |
| 조동사 can, must, should | Can you help? / You must go. |
| 현재 진행형 | She is reading. |
| 비교급/최상급 | bigger, the biggest |
| 전치사 심화 | in, on, at, by, for, with, without |
| 빈도 부사 | always, usually, sometimes, never |
| 접속사 기초 | and, but, or, because, so |

### Core Vocabulary (300 new words)

| Unit | Theme | Sample Words |
|------|-------|-------------|
| U01 | Shopping | price, cheap, expensive, buy, sell, receipt, size |
| U02 | Transport & Travel | bus, subway, ticket, arrive, depart, delay, platform |
| U03 | Weather | sunny, rainy, temperature, forecast, cold, warm, storm |
| U04 | Hobbies & Free Time | read, travel, music, film, sport, enjoy, weekend |
| U05 | Jobs & Routines | work, office, meeting, busy, schedule, start, finish |
| U06 | Restaurant & Food | order, menu, bill, spicy, vegetarian, dessert, taste |
| U07 | Health & Lifestyle | exercise, tired, sleep, medicine, healthy, stress |
| U08 | Home & Objects | furniture, kitchen, bedroom, clean, broken, fix, use |
| U09 | Emotions & Opinions | happy, worried, agree, disagree, think, feel, believe |
| U10 | Short Texts (Reading) | email, message, notice, schedule, invitation |

### Puzzle Types Added at A2
- **Error Correction (오류 찾기)** — find & fix the wrong word
- **Sentence Completion (문장 완성)** — write/choose ending
- **Short Reading (독해)** — 3–5 sentence passage + 2 questions
- **Speak & Record (말하기)** — Speech Recognition API prompt

### Sample Questions — A2

**Q1** `en_a2_u01_q003`  
Type: `multiple_choice`  
> "The shoes are too expensive. I can't _____ them."  
> A) sell  B) bring  C) **buy** ✓  D) wear  
Explanation (EN): "Buy" means to purchase something with money.  
설명 (KO): 'buy'는 돈을 주고 구입한다는 의미입니다.

---

**Q2** `en_a2_u05_q008`  
Type: `error_correction`  
> Find the error: "She go to work every day."  
> Answer: go → **goes**  
Explanation (EN): 3rd person singular (she/he/it) adds -s to the verb in simple present.  
설명 (KO): 3인칭 단수 주어(she/he/it)는 현재형 동사에 -s를 붙입니다.

---

**Q3** `en_a2_u10_q002`  
Type: `reading`  
> Passage: "Hi Amy, I'm having a party on Saturday at 7 PM. Can you come? Please let me know by Friday. — Tom"  
> Q: What does Tom want Amy to do?  
> A) Cook dinner  B) **Reply by Friday** ✓  C) Come on Friday  D) Call Tom  
Explanation (EN): Tom asks Amy to "let him know" (= reply) before Friday.  
설명 (KO): Tom은 Amy에게 금요일까지 답장해 달라고 요청하고 있습니다.

## B1 — Intermediate (친숙한 주제 표현)

### Learning Goals
- 여행, 경험, 계획에 대해 설명
- 현재완료, 가정법 기초 (if + would)
- 간접화법 (reported speech) 기초
- 어휘 1,200단어 누적
- 200–300단어 텍스트 이해

### Core Grammar
| Topic | Examples |
|-------|---------|
| 현재완료 (경험/결과) | I have visited Japan. |
| 현재완료 진행 | She has been working since 9. |
| 수동태 기초 | The book was written in 1990. |
| 조건문 1형식 | If it rains, I will stay home. |
| 관계대명사 who/which/that | the man who called |
| 간접화법 | He said that he was tired. |
| 부정사/동명사 구별 | enjoy doing / want to do |
| 접속사 심화 | although, however, therefore, unless |

### Unit Themes (12 units)
U01 Travel Experiences · U02 Work & Career · U03 Environment & Nature  
U04 Technology & Daily Life · U05 Health & Medicine · U06 Culture & Traditions  
U07 News & Media · U08 Relationships · U09 Education & Learning  
U10 Money & Economy · U11 Entertainment · U12 Mixed Reading & Listening

### Puzzle Types Added at B1
- **Passage Reconstruction (단락 재구성)** — reorder 4–5 sentences
- **Dictation (받아쓰기)** — Web Speech reads, learner types
- **Gap Text (연속 빈칸)** — 3–4 blanks in one passage
- **Discussion Prompt (말하기 응답)** — open-ended spoken answer

### Sample Questions — B1

**Q1** `en_b1_u01_q005`  
Type: `multiple_choice`  
> "I _____ to five countries before I was 30."  
> A) visit  B) visited  C) **have visited** ✓  D) am visiting  
Explanation (EN): We use present perfect for life experiences up to now.  
설명 (KO): 살면서 지금까지의 경험을 말할 때 현재완료(have + p.p.)를 씁니다.

---

**Q2** `en_b1_u04_q011`  
Type: `error_correction`  
> "This app was create by a Korean developer."  
> Answer: create → **created**  
Explanation (EN): Passive voice requires "was/were + past participle."  
설명 (KO): 수동태는 'was/were + 과거분사' 형태입니다.

---

**Q3** `en_b1_u12_q003`  
Type: `reading`  
> Passage (excerpt): "Remote work has changed how people think about offices. Many employees now prefer flexible schedules over fixed hours, and companies are redesigning workplaces to match this trend."  
> Q: What is the main idea?  
> A) Offices are disappearing  
> B) **Work habits are changing due to remote work** ✓  
> C) Employees dislike their offices  
> D) Companies are saving money  
설명 (KO): 재택근무로 인해 직원들의 근무 방식과 회사의 공간 설계가 변화하고 있다는 내용입니다.

## B2 — Upper-Intermediate (복잡한 주제 이해)

### Learning Goals
- 추상적, 사회적 주제에 대한 의견 표현
- 가정법 2·3형식, 혼합 가정법
- 복문 구조, 분사구문
- 어휘 2,000단어 누적
- 400–600단어 텍스트 분석

### Core Grammar
| Topic | Examples |
|-------|---------|
| 가정법 2형식 | If I had money, I would travel. |
| 가정법 3형식 | If I had studied, I would have passed. |
| 분사구문 | Finishing her work, she left. |
| 강조 구문 | It was John who called. |
| 도치 | Never have I seen this. |
| 명사절/부사절 심화 | what you said / so that he could |
| 관계부사 | the place where, the day when |
| 논문/에세이 표현 | Furthermore, In contrast, Nevertheless |

### Unit Themes (12 units)
U01 Society & Inequality · U02 Science & Innovation · U03 Global Issues  
U04 Arts & Creativity · U05 Law & Ethics · U06 Business & Negotiation  
U07 History & Politics · U08 Psychology & Behavior · U09 Urban Life  
U10 Sustainability · U11 Academic Reading · U12 Complex Listening

## C1 — Advanced (유창하고 자연스러운 표현)

### Learning Goals
- 뉘앙스, 함축적 의미, 유머 이해
- 관용어, 콜로케이션, 학술어 구사
- 긴 텍스트(700단어+) 빠른 독해
- 자연스러운 구어체 vs 문어체 구분
- 어휘 3,500단어 누적

### Core Grammar / Style
- 복합 수동 구문: is said to have been
- 추측 조동사: must have, can't have, should have
- 콜로케이션: make a decision / take a risk
- 관용어: bite the bullet, on the fence
- 레지스터 전환 (formal ↔ informal)
- 담화 표지: in light of, as a result, to put it differently

### Unit Themes (10 units)
U01 Idioms & Phrasal Verbs · U02 Academic Collocations · U03 Debate & Argumentation  
U04 Complex Narratives · U05 Satire & Humor · U06 Legal & Formal Writing  
U07 Media Literacy · U08 Cross-cultural Communication · U09 Nuance & Implication  
U10 C1 Comprehensive Practice

---

## C2 — Mastery (원어민 수준 정확성)

### Learning Goals
- 문체와 어조 세밀하게 조율
- 모호한 텍스트, 시, 풍자 해석
- 즉흥 발화에서도 정확성 유지
- 어휘 5,000단어+ (passive), 전문 어휘
- IELTS 8.5+ / TOEFL 110+ 수준

### Topics
- Rhetorical devices (metaphor, irony, understatement)
- Rare/literary vocabulary in context
- Ambiguous meaning resolution
- Translation nuance (EN ↔ KO)
- Authentic text: editorial, poem, speech excerpt analysis

### Supported `type` values
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `multiple_choice` | 4-choice single answer |
| `word_order` | Drag-and-drop sentence assembly |
| `fill_in_blank` | Single blank, choose from options |
| `error_correction` | Identify and fix the error |
| `reading` | Passage + 1–3 comprehension questions |
| `matching` | Connect word pairs |
| `dictation` | Listen (TTS) and type the sentence |
| `speaking_prompt` | Open-ended spoken response (Speech API) |
| `gap_text` | Multiple blanks in one passage |
| `sentence_reorder` | Reorder 4–5 sentences into a paragraph |
